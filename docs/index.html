This space is meant to be one of reinvetion, rejuvination, and of course: renewal. Twenty-one years is not that long of a time to be alive, but it is long enough to learn some things. One of the things I have learned is that you can't be afraid of change. This website is dedicated to sharing all the different ways I have worked on constantly renewing my heart, soul, and mind. In the spirit of renewal, this page will always be changing. So, if you come to this site one day and then back the next and it is completely different understand that that is kind of the point and I hope you can join me on this journey of renewal, each and every day. - Garrett
